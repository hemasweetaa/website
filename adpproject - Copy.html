ABOUT THE DANCE COMPETITION


Dancers can enter with videos they already have in their possession, provided that they have been filmed within the last year.
Choreographers may enter with choreography that has been created within the last ten years.
Finalists are presented in a Gallery of Finalists to an online audience of tens of thousands of people.
The Gallery is also open to the public to vote on their favorites themselves.

AN INTERNATIONAL AUDITION


We invite agents and other casting entities to watch finalists in the Gallery of Finalists, particularly from the Professional Category and the Choreography Division, with an eye out for talent to cast in future projects, either on stage or in video, TV, or film.
Past entities that have been invited to "shop" for dance and choreography talent through the IODC in the past include Celebrity Cruises, International Casting Agency, RWS Entertainment Group, Norwegian Cruise Line, Bloc Agency, Love Rudeye (now 3RINGS), film producers, and more.
Choreography Online also does casting for various companies in the realms of live theater and film
