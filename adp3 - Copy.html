AUDITION 2023 ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA

There are certain key conditions and eligibility criteria that a candidate must meet to be selected for the dance India dance 2023 auditions. These are as follows:

  1. Participants must be in the age group of 14 to 35 years.
  2. Participants must be a citizen of India.
  3. They should possess a medical certificate stating that they have no major health issues and must be physically and mentally fit to audition for the TV show.
  4. They must bring their songs to the audition.
  5. The participant must not have any existing criminal records.
  6. Other terms and conditions along with eligibility criteria are mentioned on the official website of Dance India Dance 2023.

SELECTION PROCESS IN AUDITION FOR DANCE INDIA DANCE

  • To be selected for the show, the candidate has to pass two auditions.
  • The first auditions are the open auditions in which three judges check the dancers brief routines to select a few from the thousands who apply for the mega auditions which are part two.
  • Mega auditions are a multiple-day process in which the 100 selected participants from the first audition perform and a competition is held among them from which 36 competitors are chosen for the show.
  • Once you enter the show there is tough competition within the show stages as well as the show progresses because many competitors who failed to perform to the expectations are eliminated.
